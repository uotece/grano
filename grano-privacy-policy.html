Privacy Policy

Grano Finance Tracker (“Grano”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information when you use Grano.

1. Data We Handle

Personal data you provide (e.g., email for account creation) is used to authenticate you via providers like Apple/Firebase. We do not sell your data.

Financial records you enter (expenses, categories, notes) are stored locally on your device and sync to cloud backends.

Diagnostic data (crash/usage logs) may be collected to improve app stability and performance.

2. Payments & Subscriptions

Purchases and subscriptions are processed by Apple via In‑App Purchase. We do not receive or store your full payment details. Subscription status may be validated via RevenueCat solely to manage entitlements (e.g., “Pro” access).

3. How We Use Data

  • Provide and improve app features (e.g., budgeting, CSV import, analytics).
  • Authenticate users and restore purchases.
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues and abuse.

4. Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share minimal data with service providers (e.g., authentication, analytics, purchase validation) strictly to operate the app and only as necessary.

5. Data Retention

We retain data as long as needed to provide the service. You can delete data at any time, either through account settings or by contacting us.

6. Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

7. International Transfers

Depending on your location and the location of our providers, your data may be processed in other countries with different data protection laws.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. We may provide in‑app notice of material changes.

9. Contact

Questions or requests (including data deletion): hello@usegrano.com
