Welcome to the support page for Grano Finance Tracker. Here you’ll find help with common questions and ways to contact us if you need more assistance.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add an expense?
Open the app, tap the + button, enter your expense details, and save. This works for free users without requiring a subscription.

How do I unlock Pro features?
Pro features are available as optional in-app subscriptions (monthly or yearly). You can subscribe or restore purchases anytime from the “Unlock Pro” screen.

How do I cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions on your iPhone to cancel.
